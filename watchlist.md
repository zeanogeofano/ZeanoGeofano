## Add/Remove Stocks
- Use the **Add Stock** button to add a stock to your watchlist by entering the stock ticker symbol.
- To remove a stock, click the **Remove** button next to the stock entry.

## Sorting Options
You can sort the stocks in your watchlist by:
- **Performance**: Sort by stock performance percentage.
- **Price**: Sort by current stock price.
- **Volume**: Sort by trading volume.

## Favorite Stocks
Mark your favorite stocks with a star icon. Click the star to toggle your favorite status.

## Real-Time Price Tracking
The prices displayed in the watchlist will update in real-time, ensuring you have the latest information.

## Portfolio Alerts
Set alerts for specific stocks based on performance thresholds or price changes. Notifications will keep you informed of significant movements.
